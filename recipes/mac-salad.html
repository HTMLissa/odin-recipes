Mac Salad

Description:

This Classic Hawaiian Macaroni Salad recipe has a delicious creamy dressing thatâ€™s tangy, sweet, and totally addicting! It tastes just like it came from a Hawaiian Food Truck.

You can prepare this Hawaiian mac salad for up to 3 days in advance. Just follow the complete instructions below, transfer the mixture to an airtight container, and keep it in the refrigerator.

Ingredients:

  • 500g elbow macaroni
  • 200ml milk
  • 200ml cream
  • 400g mayonnaise
  • 1 1/2 tabespoons grated yellow onion
  • 2 teaspoons pepper
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoont dark brown sugar
  • 60 ml apple cider vinegar
  • 5 green onions
  • 2 large celery ribs
  • 150-200g grated carrots

Steps:

  1. In a medium bowl whisk together the milk, cream, mayonnaise, grated onion, dark brown sugar, 1 teaspoon of salt, and 2 teaspoons of pepper until smooth. Refrigerate until needed.
  2. Cook the macaroni as per the package instructions, except add 5 minutes to the cooking time on the package instructions (you want the macaroni to be very soft and plump so it absorbs the dressing better).
  3. Drain the macaroni into a colander and then dump it back into the pot. Add in the apple cider vinegar and stir until the pasta absorbs the vinegar. Cover the pot with a lid and cool for 20 minutes at room temperature.
  4. Add half of the dressing and stir to combine. Then, cover the lid and cool for another 20 minutes.
  5. Add in the remaining dressing, green onions, celery, and carrots to the pot and stir to combine, and season with salt and pepper if needed.
  6. Cool completely to room temperature, about 30 more minutes.
  7. Transfer the macaroni salad to a bowl and cover with plastic wrap. Chill in the refrigerator for at least 2 hours before serving.
