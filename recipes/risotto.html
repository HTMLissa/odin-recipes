Homemade Mushroom Risotto - the perfect early fall recipe

Outside it is slowly getting colder, while the leaves shine in the most colorful way. Sandals are exchanged for boots and the sun loses intensity as the days get shorter.

Autumn - the golden season. Now it's time to warm up again from the inside as well. Perfect timing for a delicious mushroom risotto. Whether you pick them yourself or get them from the supermarket, this easy recipe always works and makes every autumn day a good one.

Ingredients

  • 500g mushrooms
  • 3 shallot onions
  • 400g risotto rice
  • 80g butter
  • 1 bunch of parsley
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 200ml white wine
  • 800ml chicken broth
  • 250g parmesan cheese - freshly grated
  • olive oil
  • salt & pepper
  • nutmeg
